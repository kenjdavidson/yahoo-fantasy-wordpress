Yahoo! Plugin Details

In order to use the Yahoo! Fantasy Plugin shortcodes throughout your posts and pages, you need to first login to Yahoo! and provide your authorization for this application to access your Fantasy details. In order to complete the login, follow the link below. You'll be taken to the Yahoo! login page; provide your login details and click to continue.

User Account

Login to Yahoo!

You've successfully logged into Yahoo! Services. Please confirm your account information below. If you want to remove the Yahoo! Authorization, you can click the logout button. Note - logging out will cause all your pages and posts to stop displaying Yahoo! Fantasy details.
